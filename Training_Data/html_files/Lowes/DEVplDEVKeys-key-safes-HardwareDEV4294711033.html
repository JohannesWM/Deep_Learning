Frequently Asked Questions


Q: Where Is the Best Place to Put a Key Safe?

A: Key safes can be mounted directly on top of your front door for convenient and secure access to your home, workshop, garage or guest house.


Q: Is a Key or Digital Safe Better?

A: Both a physical key and a digital safe provide highly effective security. Whether you prefer to maintain your key in a safe place or to remember a number combination to open your safe will determine which one suits your home better.


Q: How Does a Lock Box Work?

A: A lock box is typically installed over your door handle. A locked storage compartment provides space for securing your house keys. Unlock the compartment using a number combination to gain access to the keys so you can open your door.
