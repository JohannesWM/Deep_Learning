Choosing the Right Knee Pads


Whether you’re a pro or do-it-yourselfer, safety and comfort are essential for any project. At Lowe’s, we offer all the gear you need — such as knee pads — to protect yourself as you handle your household or work tasks.


Knee pads for work are constructed with foam, gel pads or hard plastic to accommodate different surfaces. Gel knee pads can help with knee pain and fatigue and may be ideal if you work on your knees for long periods of time. Foam knee pads are another good choice, but some types might not offer as much comfort or protection as gel.


Whether you need heavy-duty workers knee pads, such as flooring knee pads, or gardening knee pads for lighter tasks outdoors, we can help you find the right pair. At Lowe’s, we also have kids knee pads to cushion little knees while children help in the garden. Knee and elbow pads can help kids avoid scrapes and bruises during playtime, too.

Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What’s better — gel or foam knee pads?


A: Both foam and gel knee pads can provide excellent cushioning and protect your joints if you work on your knees for long periods of time. The choice of gel versus foam largely comes down to preference. Some types and brands of gel knee pads may provide more cushioning than foam pads because gel pads feature a combination of foam and gel. Foam knee pads use only foam.


Q: Should I wear knee pads at work?


A: Wearing knee pads is a good idea if your job requires you to put strain on your knees. For example, if you lay flooring or do landscaping for a living, knee protection could make your life easier — both now and in the future. Repeatedly putting weight on your knees for long periods of time can lead to health issues, such as bursitis, a painful joint condition. The right workwear, including knee pads, can help.


Q: How do I choose knee pads?


A: On Lowes.com, you can shop by knee pad type. This makes it easier to find the right pair of knee pads for your needs. For example, if you work in flooring and need knee pads with gel for extra support, filter your search for flooring knee pads specifically.
