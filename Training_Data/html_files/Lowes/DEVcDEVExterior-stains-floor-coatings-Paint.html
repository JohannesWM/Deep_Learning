One-Stop Shopping for Your Exterior Painting Needs 

At Lowe’s, we offer a range of exterior stains, paints and floor coatings to help you enhance and safeguard decks, patios, porches, garden beds, benches, garage floors and basement floors.


Update Your Garage Floor With a Garage Floor Coating

Painting your garage floor makes your space feel fresh and clean. It’s a simple, budget-friendly update that’ll give you impressive results.


Select a garage floor epoxy from the variety of sheens and colors we offer to customize your look. Or choose a clear epoxy to protect your floor without changing its appearance. Epoxy makes concrete more chip-resistant and durable. Also, unlike plain concrete floors, painted floors have extra protection against mold, mildew and automobile fluids. Plus, they’re easy to clean and don’t produce the dust that bare concrete floors do.


Once you’ve painted your garage floor, you can continue the room’s transformation by adding new storage containers and a wall-mounted garage storage system.
 

Paint for Your Basement Floor

Instantly add more usable square footage to your home when you paint your concrete basement floor. At Lowe’s, we carry concrete paint from brands you know and trust — including Ghostshield, Valspar® and Seal-Krete — to help you achieve a beautiful finish.


Before painting, fill any cracks and holes with a concrete filler. Then sand the area to get rid of any rough spots, using a sanding block for small areas and a power sander for large areas. Consider a stain and sealer in one to give your floor extra protection.


Always make sure the area where you’ll be painting has good ventilation. Also, protect yourself from dust and debris by wearing a mask and safety glasses.


Wood Stain for Outdoor Structures

Improve the look of your deck or other outdoor structures with exterior wood stain. We carry a variety of gorgeous wood stain colors so you can create the look you want.


Before you start your project, prepare the wood by using a wood cleaner. After applying deck stain, be sure to use a sealant to keep out moisture and prevent fungal growth and wood rot.


No matter what look you want to achieve, we have the materials and resources to help you. Shop Lowes.com today to get started.


Frequently Asked Questions


Q: How long does epoxy last on a garage floor?

A: Several factors determine the life span of an epoxy floor coating, including the amount of foot and vehicle traffic the floor sees, the thickness of the coating and the kind of maintenance the floor receives. Generally, though, epoxy can last up to 10 years, if not longer.


Q: What kind of paint should I use on my garage floor?

A: For the best results, choose an epoxy paint specifically designed for garage floors. At Lowe’s, we carry a wide selection of garage floor epoxy and paint to ensure you can find the best option for your garage floor.


Q: What kind of stain is best for a deck?

A: Adding any kind of exterior wood stain helps protect your deck from mold, mildew, moisture and sun damage. Ultimately, personal preference determines which stain is best for you. If you want to highlight the natural look of your wood deck, choosing a clear, transparent or semi-transparent stain will allow the wood grain to show through. At Lowe’s, we also have opaque options that completely cover wood grain and transform your deck’s appearance.
