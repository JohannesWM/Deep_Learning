Listen to Tunes With Jobsite Radios

A jobsite radio helps to make your project more enjoyable. Whether you’re working on the roof or loading up the truck for a day on the jobsite, being able to stream your favorite music or podcasts can keep you energized and productive. And a day of yardwork goes much quicker while you listen to tunes. At Lowe’s, we carry a range of cordless radios that offer convenience and performance.


If you make these radios part of your everyday tool kit, you might want to add tough protective roll cages that can help the radios stand up to accidental kicks and bumps — common where workers are coming and going. Connect your radio to a Bluetooth device with a range of up to 100 feet, and you can enjoy high-quality speakers and static-free reception throughout the day.


Auxiliary inputs let you simultaneously charge your phone or other mobile devices while you listen to your radio. You’ll find our best jobsite radios also offer such features as AM/FM radio, alternating-current (AC) power cords, USB ports and dual subwoofers for stereo sound to make your music or podcasts more enjoyable.
