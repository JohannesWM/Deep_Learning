Create an Accessible Kitchen That's Safe for Your Whole Family


If you're looking for products to create an ADA compliant kitchen, Lowe's has appliances, accessories and cabinet parts to help make it happen. Whether you're building a wheelchair-accessible kitchen or catering towards other accessibility needs, you're sure to find what you need. Lowe's can help with installation, too.


Large appliances such as refrigerators, dishwashers, and washers and dryers are essential tools in the home, so ensure they're easy to use for every member of your family. Filter by the type of appliance you're looking for on Lowes.com to create your accessible kitchen. We offer accessible refrigerators, laundry appliances, dishwashers and more. Make sure no one struggles with cabinet doors or drawers with accessible cabinet hardware.


Make everything in your kitchen, from prep to cleanup, accessible with helpful accessories and fixtures. Install a touch-activated faucet that can be conveniently turned on and off without the use of a handle. Put nonslip kitchen floor mats in busy areas to prevent slips and falls. You may also want to consider placing anti-fatigue mats in front of an accessible kitchen sink, countertop or another area where you stand for long periods of time.
