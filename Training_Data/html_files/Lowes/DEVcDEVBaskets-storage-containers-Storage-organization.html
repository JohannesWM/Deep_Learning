Tackle the Chaos With Storage Solutions

When it comes to organizing day-to-day essentials, storing seasonal items or transforming an area into an organized and functional space, the right basket or storage container can quickly corral the mess. From plastic containers to woven baskets to fabric bins, Lowe’s has a variety of storage options to suit your needs.

Size
It’s important that the storage solution you purchase fits everything you want packed away, so size is a big factor to consider when shopping. Large storage tubs or totes are perfect for garage organization, allowing you to store gardening supplies, extension cords and tools, or for closet organization to keep extra blankets or pillows tucked out of the way. If you need something smaller for things, like extra notebooks and school supplies or lotions and other bath products, plastic bins and wire baskets are practical choices.

Color
Clear storage bins are always a great option; no matter what you store inside you can easily see the contents. This type of container is also more practical than stylish, so it may be best used behind closed doors. If you’re looking for something that can be used in plain sight, consider colorful bins — perfect for playrooms — or fabric options with fun patterns to spice up your organization game. Decorative storage is just as functional as practical storage, and it allows you to enhance your home’s aesthetic while keeping things in their place.

Types
Just from size and color alone we know that storage containers come in a wide range of options. So, consider what type of storage you need for what you’re storing and where it’ll be stored. If you’re planning on utilizing the space under your bed, underbed storage containers are perfect for holding seasonal clothes, shoes or blankets. In the closet, stackable storage bins can help save shelf space and are easy to move around if you need to rework the space. And don’t forget about material. With options for plastic storage containers in metal, wood or fabric, you can find one to meet your needs in every way.

A simple storage basket can transform the way a room feels, freeing it of disorder and creating a sense of calm by giving your belongings a place of their own. Lowe’s carries trusted brands, like Rubbermaid and Sterilite, so you can confidently get organized and enjoy life without clutter.
