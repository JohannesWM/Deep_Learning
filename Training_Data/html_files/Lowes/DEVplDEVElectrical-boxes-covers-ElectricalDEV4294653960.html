Keep Wire Connections Safe With Electrical Box Covers


Electrical outlet boxes, also called electrical boxes, help to make power easily and safely available in homes and businesses. Electrical box covers are necessary to protect both users and the wiring inside. Available in a wide variety of sizes, materials and colors, they can be a practical and stylish addition to walls and rooms.


Where wiring comes together from several lines, a junction box is used. Like an electrical box but without the outlet, a junction box cover protects the enclosed wires and helps to prevent accidental contact.


For outdoor electrical safety, electrical box extenders help to make outlets flush with exterior walls made of brick or siding. Outdoor outlet cover boxes are great when you’re using extension cords for inflatables and holiday decorations.


At the center of your home’s electrical system is the electrical panel box, which houses the breakers for all wiring. At Lowe’s, we offer panel boxes from quality vendors like RACO. We also have electrical boxes from CANTEX to go with BELL outlets to complete any wiring project you may have.
