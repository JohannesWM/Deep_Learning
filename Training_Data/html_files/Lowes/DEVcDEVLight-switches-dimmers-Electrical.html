Light Switches & Dimmers

Lighting Control at Your Fingertips

Whether you're replacing your home's light switches or picking out what you want to install in a new build, Lowe's has the right light switch to fit your specifications. Browse light switches by types and features on our site.


Light Switches by Type

If you have a strong preference about the kind of switch you want to use throughout your home, you can filter by type on Lowes.com. We carry traditional switch styles such as rocker and toggle switches, but we also offer tap, push and touchless switches. Find switches that work with the way the lighting is wired in your home and how you want to control your lights. Choose from 3-way and 4-way switches that allow you to control your lighting from several locations, and multi-location light switches that make it possible to have control over a light from multiple switches. Some also include dimming features for added convenience.


Consider remote control switches and dimmers if you want to be able to adjust the lights without getting up to go to a switch. Use a motion sensor light switch to turn on your lights as soon as you enter a room. Wireless switches let you add a way to control a light without worrying about additional electrical wiring. If you need switches for your whole home, look for 10-packs of identical switches to make your shopping easier.


Light Switch Features and Parts

Interested in syncing light switches with your connected home devices? Choose a smart light switch that's compatible with the technology you already have, such as Amazon Alexa. Never struggle to find a light switch in the dark again with illuminated switches that light up when the light is turned off. For ceiling fans, be sure to use a switch that also has fan control capabilities.


Fix any broken switches or dimmers with replacement parts from Lowe's. We carry parts like rocker plates, knobs and slide levers so you can get your light switch working like new again. Browse items from popular brands such as Lutron or GE to find the perfect part. If you're partial to a particular line of switches created by a brand, you can filter by collection as well.


Types of Light Dimmers

Shop for dimmer switches based on the type of light bulb you have in your light fixture. We carry dimmers compatible with halogen, incandescent, xenon, fluorescent, CFL and HID bulbs. You can find dimmer switches, kits, companion dimmers and master dimmers on our website and in stores. You can also filter your search for a programmable dimmer switch that can adjust your lighting based on the time of day.


Dimmers, like regular light switches, also come in a variety of styles including rocker, decorator, slide, toggle, touch square and rotary. Lowe's has many dimmer switch colors to choose from so you can find the perfect match for your home dcor or wall paint colors. Explore colors like terracotta, bluestone and plum to coordinate your dimmers in every room.
