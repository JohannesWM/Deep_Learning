Keep Your Eyes Protected With Safety Glasses From Lowe’s

Whether your project is around the house or on a jobsite, you need the right eye protection. Protective eyewear can keep your eyes safe from hazards including dust, chemicals, radiation and flying objects. At Lowe’s, we have some of the best safety glasses, face shields and safety goggles to choose from, so you can get the option that’s right for you.
 

Depending on your project, a standard pair of safety glasses might offer plenty of protection. But if you’re working in a particularly hazardous environment, like a welding shop, you may want to consider specialized options like welding goggles or even a welding helmet. Safety glasses with side shields are another option for additional protection.
 

Protective eyewear can come with an array of special features. For example, anti-fog safety glasses have a coating that keeps the lenses clear. And if you spend most of your day outside, consider polarized safety glasses that reduce glare and ease eye stress and fatigue.


Bifocal safety glasses and safety glasses with readers are options that combine eye protection and a vision aid in each pair. But if eyewear is part of your everyday wardrobe, you may want to look for safety glasses that you can wear over your regular glasses. You can stay comfortable in your usual pair while adding the protection that everyday glasses don’t have.


Whatever your needs, safety glasses come in a variety of styles and are an essential part of your personal protective equipment kit.
