Deliver Water or Gas to Your Appliances With a Supply Line


A supply line is a flexible metal or plastic hose that delivers water or gas from your main line to an individual fixture such as an appliance or faucet. Our selection includes appliance supply lines and drain hoses, faucet and toilet supply lines, appliance outlet boxes, and water supply line connectors and accessories.


For just about any appliance that requires water or gas, there's a supply line available. Browse a variety of supply lines for washing machines, steam dryers, dishwashers, ice makers, dehumidifiers, water heaters, water softeners and more. Gas connectors are used for gas stoves or other gas-powered appliances, while faucet and toilet supply lines deliver water to faucets and toilets.


Supply line connectors and accessories include all the fittings you'll need for installation including valves, add-a-tee, nipples, couplings, compression nuts, shank nuts, washers, stiffeners and more. Finally, appliance outlet boxes are a handy all-in-one box for appliances that require supply valves such as washing machines and ice makers. These boxes give you an easy shut-off valve to protect against accidental leaks when the appliances aren't in use.
