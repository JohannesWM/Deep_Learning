Sleek and Modern Kitchen & Pantry Cabinets


Kitchen Cabinetry

Selecting kitchen cabinets involves weighing a number of considerations, including storage, style and space. Lowe's has the kitchen pantry cabinets, base &amp; corner cabinets, and wall cabinets you need to complete the kitchen you've always wanted. With a range of kitchen cabinet colors and designs, you can customize your kitchen to look the way you imagine it. Plus, Lowe's can help you with useful inspirational articles so you can begin bringing your kitchen project or remodel to life.


Our Kitchen Planning Guide will help you to create a budget and know what to expect as you get started.


Base and Sink Base Cabinets

Cabinets that need to be topped with a sink or countertop are called base cabinets. Since they're built without a top, you're able to customize their covering. Drop-in sinks tend to be easy to install, and a variety of countertops that complement your cabinets are available.


Base cabinets come in a range of sizes, so it's important to know your space's precise dimensions. Base cabinet doors also come in different styles. For instance, square doors are a popular option. Explore the features and finishes of our base cabinets to get an idea of what you'd like to see in your kitchen.


Corner, Wall and Kitchen Pantry Cabinets

There are cabinets to fill every space in your kitchen, including cabinets designed specifically for corners. Wall cabinets are mounted to sit above your counter or sink, while a kitchen pantry cabinet is usually larger and freestanding. If you only need to update or replace a wall cabinet door, you can buy these individually rather than installing an entirely new cabinet.


Fully assembled pantry cabinets, kitchen wall cabinets and other cabinetry are also available. Buying your cabinets already assembled will save you time and effort, and make your project move more quickly.


Kitchen Cabinet Hardware and Accessories

Deciding which handles, faucets and hardware to install can be just as exciting as picking out the cabinets themselves. Drawer pulls, cabinet knobs and other hardware can accent your chosen cabinets either to blend in or stand out. Value packs will help you save money, and they provide you with enough pieces to equip multiple cabinets. Kitchen knobs allow you to be very expressive, with styles that include modern, round and novelty options.


If you need crown moulding or panels, these are available in finishes to match your chosen cabinets. Lowe's also has products for kitchen and pantry organization, such as drawer organizers, spice racks and food storage containers. Keeping your kitchen in order will help you find things when you need them, and make your space look and feel tidy.
