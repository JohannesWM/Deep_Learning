Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What types of mold and mildew removers does Lowes sell?

A: We carry liquid and powder mold and mildew removers. The most popular option is a liquid spray mold remover. If you have a large area infected by mold, you may want to consider a gallon jug, as opposed to the typical 32-ounce container of liquid mold remover.


Q: Can I test for mold myself?

A: Yes, you can test for mold yourself with one of our mold test kits. These kits include items necessary for effective mold testing including petri dishes, swabs and mold growth medium.


Q: Is it possible to remove mold completely?

A: Yes, you can remove mold completely with a store-bought mold remover or an alternative, such as bleach or hydrogen peroxide. Once you remove mold from the area, consider using a mold inhibitor, which can prevent the growth of new mold. To learn how to remove mold yourself, check out our guide on the subject, Mold Removal on Hard Surfaces.
