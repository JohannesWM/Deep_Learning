Have Fun With a Recreational Vehicle

Whether you’re new to power sports or have enjoyed them for decades, the appeal of a shiny new utility terrain vehicle (UTV) or dirt bike never fades. At Lowe’s, we carry a selection of recreational vehicles that’ll suit every type of power sports enthusiast.


Utility terrain vehicles, which some people also call utility task vehicles, are great for hauling and towing equipment to and from hard-to-reach places that trucks and cars can’t access. Many UTVs at Lowe’s, such as the Massimo UTVs, come in either two-wheel or four-wheel drive systems so users can maximize full engine power for hauling, towing or the trails. And with additional accessories, UTVs can also be used for plowing snow and fields, spreading seeds and raking large areas.


Lowe’s also carries accessories for single-rider all-terrain vehicles (ATVs), such as tire chains and Bluetooth speakers. Whatever type of off-road vehicle you have, you can outfit it with proper safety and performance gear.


If you’re looking to purchase a dirt bike, it’s best to first think about what you need or want it for. Motocross bikes are designed for racing and are great for high-rev and high-speed situations. They’re also generally more high maintenance. We also carry minibikes sized for young ones. They’re great for beginners and teens who want to learn to ride off-road.
