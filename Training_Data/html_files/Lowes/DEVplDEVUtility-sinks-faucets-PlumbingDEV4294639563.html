Add Versatility to Your Home With a Utility Sink


When you're not near your bathroom or kitchen sink and need a place for running water, a utility sink is a handy solution. Our selection includes a variety of stainless steel utility sinks and other materials such as polypropylene, cast iron or vitreous china. Utility sinks can be freestanding or wall-mounted. They're usually undermount or self-rimming for easy cleanup. 


A utility sink can be great in your laundry room or mudroom for taking care of dirty clothes and shoes as soon as possible. It's also handy near the garage when you need a water supply for washing the car or filling up a mop bucket. Some products include the faucet, sink and cabinet, while others only include the sink, so be sure to look for what's right for you.


In addition to sinks, we've got standalone utility sink faucets for repair or replacement projects. Browse single-handle and double-handle faucets from top brands such as KOHLER, Moen, Delta, American Standard, Elkay and more in a variety of finishes.
