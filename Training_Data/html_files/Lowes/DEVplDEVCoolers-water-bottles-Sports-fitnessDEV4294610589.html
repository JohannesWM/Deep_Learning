At Lowes, we have a wide selection of coolers with and without wheels as well as travel mugs, water bottles and other insulated items.

 

Choose a four-wheeled cart or luggage-style cooler. Or consider a two-wheeled model that you tilt and roll. We also offer an extensive assortment of other coolers in a variety of sizes, materials and designs, including backpack coolers. Check out our informative guide, called How to Choose the Best Cooler, to learn more.


Our water bottle and mug selection includes options in nearly any size, style and color imaginable. Choose from a number of containers that are insulated, dishwasher-safe and free of bisphenol A (BPA). We also have wine tumblers with spill-resistant lids. And just as you can with coolers, you can choose a bottle, mug or tumbler sporting the logo and colors of your favorite professional or college team.
