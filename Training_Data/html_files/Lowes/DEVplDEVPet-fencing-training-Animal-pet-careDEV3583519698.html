Keep Pets Safe with an Electric Pet Fence


Invisible pet fences provide a boundary for pet safety without installing an actual fence. These pet fences are comprised of a receiver and transmitter unit, with the dog collar acting as the receiver. When a pet gets close to the boundary, the collar deploys a number of correctional measures such as a beeping noise or a mild electric shock. 


Pet fence transmitters come in different variations like wireless in-house types that are protected from the weather. Underground units function with a boundary wire buried around the yard and allow flexible use around obstacles of any dimension. Check out our guide — Install an Electronic Dog Fence — to learn how to choose and set up an electric fence system. Also, shop our long-lasting dog training shock collar batteries to keep your wireless pet fence functional every day. 


A physical chain-link, metal or wooden fence could also fall victim to pets digging underneath, inclement weather or pest damage. An electric fence barrier, which is rust-proof, makes it much harder for pets to dig out or damage the fence itself.
