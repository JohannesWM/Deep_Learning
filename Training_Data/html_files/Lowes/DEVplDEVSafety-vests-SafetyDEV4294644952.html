Frequently Asked Questions


Q: Whats a reflective safety vest?

A: People use reflective safety vests for increased visibility in working conditions where there are low light levels or when working in a nighttime environment. The vests come in a range of materials, including polyester, nylon and modacrylic/aramid. The varying levels of reflectivity enable enhanced visibility and high visibility.


Q: What are the different classes of safety vests?

A: Safety vests fall into three classes, based on the amount and placement of reflective tape and background color usually yellow, orange or lime on the vest. Class 1 safety vests are for individuals who work in environments where traffic flow is 25 mph or slower and in other low-impact areas. Class 2 safety vests are for use in areas with low visibility, especially visibility reduced by weather, and in areas of heavier traffic over 25 mph. High-traffic areas require a Class 3 vest, which typically has reflective sleeves. A Class 3 vest contains the most reflective striping.
