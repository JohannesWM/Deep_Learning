Protect Your Family With Child Safety Products


Create a safe home for your family with child safety accessories from Lowe's. Child safety cabinet locks secure kitchen and bathroom cabinets, keeping curious youngsters away from potentially dangerous items like household cleaners or beauty products. Pair these locks with spring-loaded drawer latches that keep kids out of drawers but are easy for adults to open. Furniture straps allow you to secure heavy furniture to the wall to prevent children from tipping these pieces over.


Use childproof gates to block off stairs and other hazards for young children. These sturdy metal or wooden fit securely in doorways and at the top or bottom of the stairs. Choose from pressure-mounted or hardware-mounted styles depending on your home's layout.


Keep a close eye on your child while they sleep with a baby monitor camera. Find cameras capable of streaming HD video directly to your smartphone. Some styles come with two cameras, which allows you to watch your baby's room from different angles.
