Quality Results Come From the Right Flooring Tools

When it comes to getting professional results for your do-it-yourself (DIY) flooring project, using the right tools and accessories for the job makes a huge difference. That’s why Lowe’s offers everything you need from start to finish.


Getting Started

Whether you’re a weekend warrior or a seasoned professional, you’ll find the right tools for preparing and repairing a subfloor at Lowe’s. We carry floor scrapers to remove debris, skim coat to level and patch underlayment as well as underlay for laminate flooring. You’ll find plenty of options in our aisles and on Lowes.com.


Tile

When working with tile, you’ll want to have several supplies on hand to make your job easier. In addition to tile spacers, you’ll need an adhesive to hold the tile in place. While some people prefer mastic to thinset, it’s important to follow the manufacturer’s application guidelines when laying tile in a wet area, like a shower.


Ensure your workshop is stocked with a tile cutter. There are several different varieties to choose from, including manual cutters, snap cutters and wet-tile saws. Make your selection based on your experience level and project size. For example, if you’re only working with two or three tiles, you may want to use a tile nipper or jigsaw for the job. For larger projects, a tile installation kit may be the best choice. You’ll also need a tile saw, which can cut a variety of tile, including natural stone, pavers, ceramic and porcelain.


Remember to pick up tile edge trim when you’re stocking up on supplies. These pieces will help you give your project a professional and finished look.


Carpet

In addition to a leveling compound, tackless strips or carpet tack strips, you’ll want to have carpet pad, carpet tape and carpet staplers on hand. Working from the longest wall in the room, you’ll use your carpet cutter to ensure a perfect fit.


Whether you’re working with carpet, wood, laminate or tile, Lowe’s has the supplies and tools you need for a job well done. Shop with us for everything from trim and moulding to grout and tile cleaner to ensure professional results for your next flooring job.
