Stay Warm With Lowe's Selection of Space Heaters

At Lowe's, we have many types of space heaters for a warm and comfortable home. Electric wall heaters with timer settings allow hands-free appliance operation. Oscillating electric space heaters will keep multiple people warm and distribute heat. Lowe's also carries electric wall heaters with automatic shut-off features, which can help you lower utility bills if you fall asleep while it's on. No matter your preference for staying warm, shop at Lowe's to find heating appliances perfect for you and your home.


Gas Space Heaters

Gas space heaters work during an electrical blackout, are generally fuel-efficient and produce hotter heat. Those that can warm spaces up to 1,000 square feet are great for shoppers who need appliances that cover a large area. Radiant heaters make more direct contact with objects and people, which will help you warm up more quickly. Many shoppers also enjoy the reliability and economical value of liquid propane heaters, since their required fuel source is cheaper and more readily available.


Outdoor Heaters

Chilly weather shouldn't stop you from enjoying your beautiful backyard. That's why Lowe's carries all sorts of outdoor heaters, including electric-powered options. Instead of having to get up and refill your heaters with fuel, you can simply plug them in and continuously bask in their warmth. If you love the aroma of a wood fire, try a grass wood-burning fire pit, which allows for enjoying a blazing fire's warmth over grass and amidst nature. Composite outdoor fireplaces come in a variety of contemporary designs, perfect for shoppers who want a modern-looking heating source.


Garage Heaters

Most garage heaters are portable space heaters, which are handy not only in the garage but for any camping trips or for dining in your backyard on colder days. Garage heaters that come with a remote control let you adjust your heater's settings without disturbing your relaxation. Garage heaters with thermostat displays allow homeowners to set their exact desired temperature for optimal comfort. If you're concerned about energy efficiency, choose an energy-saving garage heater. Using one will ensure that you keep warm without overworking your utilities.


Kerosene Heaters

Consider our selection of kerosene heaters, which require the use of kerosene, a highly affordable and powerful fuel source. Heaters with a 10-gallon fuel capacity will store a lot more of this energy-efficient fuel source, therefore avoiding the need for constant fuel refills. Convection kerosene heaters are fantastic if you want heat distributed all around the room. If you want an appliance with a sleeker aesthetic, choose a black kerosene heater. They'll be more inconspicuous than heaters in other colors/finishes and are least prone to stains and discoloration.
