Respiratory Masks to Protect Your Airways


Respiratory masks can help prevent dust, paint fumes, and other solid or liquid aerosols from getting into your air passages. Use them on the job site, at home or anywhere you feel the need to mask up. Lowes carries a large assortment of safety masks and all-purpose face masks designed to address a host of protection needs. 


Find paint respirators to use while spray painting or whenever youre applying solvents or pesticides. We also carry safety masks for sanding and fiberglass installation. A mask with valves allows for cooler and more comfortable breathing.  


For everyday protection, we have all-purpose face masks in both disposable and cloth options. Fun designs adorn many of our masks for children, some featuring popular characters. Many of our masks include features such as adjustable straps and bendable nose pieces for a snug fit. Buy single masks or packages in a range of quantities. We also carry disposable charcoal and carbon filters that you can insert into a variety of masks for additional protection.
