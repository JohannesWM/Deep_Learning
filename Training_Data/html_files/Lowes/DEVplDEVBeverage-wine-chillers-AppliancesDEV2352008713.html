Keep Drinks Cool with Beverage and Wine Fridges

Keep cold drinks close at hand while you're entertaining guests or having a family meal. From a small wine fridge in the kitchen to a beverage cooler for your backyard, Lowe's has the right option for you. If you're entertaining, you can keep a drinkware set within easy reach so guests can help themselves.
 

Choose a wine fridge with built-in racks or a mixed beverage cooler that lets you stock wine, cans and bottled drinks. If you're a home brewer, or prefer your beer on tap, a kegerator keeps beer chilled at the perfect serving temperature. Create a home bar with a beverage fridge and ice buckets. You can even add hot and cold beverage dispensers for sparkling water or hot tea and coffee.
 

Take your pick from built-in, freestanding, under-the-counter or portable fridges. While most are designed for indoor use, some models can be used outside. Each beverage fridge has adjustable temperature controls and see-through glass doors so you can quickly check stock levels. If you need more specialized features, such as reversible or double doors, interior lighting or LED temperature displays, Lowe's has a chiller to suit your needs.
