Create a Home Gym With Fitness and Exercise Equipment


Bring your fitness goals within reach with home exercise equipment. Having your favorite fitness machine easily accessible makes it more likely that youll reach your goals because it removes the inconvenience of traveling to and from the gym. With the right home workout equipment, you can exercise whenever youve got a bit of time and motivation if the equipment is readily available.


If youre working at a desk most of the day and struggling to get enough physical activity, add an under-desk elliptical or pair a compact treadmill with a standing desk. It may be worth investing in a folding exercise bike with an attached computer desk if you find yourself working from home often. Some other popular home gym machines include foldable rowing machines, freestanding weight benches and resistance cross trainers.


Consider exercise equipment with different functions, such as a combination workout tower or an elliptical/stationary bike combo. No weights are required with Pilates machines that rely entirely on your body weight. For help finding the right workout equipment for you, see our guide on that subject, Choose the Best Elliptical, Treadmill and Fitness Equipment. For home gym ideas, including tips for setting up floor training or strength training zones, check out our how-to guide from do-it-yourself expert Monica Mangin.
