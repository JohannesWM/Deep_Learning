Protect Your Home With Hurricane Shutters


Dont wait until a storm is on the horizon to take measures to protect your home. If you live in a hurricane-prone area, permanent hurricane shutters that you can close or roll down when needed may be the best option. Hurricane panels are a cost-effective temporary alternative to keep on hand, ready to install over your windows when a storm threatens. 


Lowes carries hurricane window shutters and panels in a variety of sizes. Choose from aluminum, steel or polycarbonate. Our polycarbonate panels will allow light in while still offering impact resistance. Many of our panels install either horizontally or vertically. Depending on your location, look for panels that meet Florida Building Code (FBC), International Building Code (IBC) or Texas Department of Insurance (TDI) standards. 


Also look to Lowes for the hardware you need for your shutters or panels. We offer hardware packages that are adaptable for installation in cement, wood and masonry, as well as connectors for attaching two panels together without hardware. For tips on installing a variety of hurricane shutters and panels, consult our guide How to Install Storm Shutters.
