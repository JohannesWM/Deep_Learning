Double Hung Windows

  • Allow for both sashes to slide up and down
  • Provide ventilation from the top and bottom
  • Add safety for homes with children and pets

Single Hung Windows

  • Have a movable bottom sash and a fixed top sash
  • Provide ventilation through the bottom sash
  • Are energy efficient with minimal maintenance

Sliding Windows

  • Allow for more light and ventilation than hung windows
  • Are energy efficient and easy to use
  • May or may not have a fixed sash, depending on the model

Casement Windows

  • Have a hinge on the side and swing out, allowing for maximum ventilation
  • Open with a small crank and are great for hard-to-reach areas
