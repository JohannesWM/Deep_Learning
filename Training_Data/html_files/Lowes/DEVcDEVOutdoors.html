Outdoors Tools and Equipment for Landscaping


Improve Your Landscaping With Lowe’s

Lowe’s carries everything you need for a gorgeous and functional backyard. Want to entertain the kids in your backyard? Then consider getting a trampoline, which will keep them healthy while offering plenty of fun. Or maybe you’re looking to update your landscaping. In that case, you’ll want landscape fabric, which keeps weeds away while maintaining soil moisture. We also offer a variety of lawn mowers and other outdoor tools and equipment to clean up and transform your yard. By shopping with us, you’ll realize that we have all the landscaping products necessary to level up the exterior of your property.


Patio Furniture

Being able to relax on patio furniture will help you enjoy the outdoors in comfort and style. Recharge by taking a nap on a teak frame daybed. If you want to soak up some sun, a patio umbrella will reduce sun exposure. Perhaps you have a smaller backyard and want space-saving furniture options that are still aesthetically pleasing. In that case, take a look at our bistro furniture sets. Lowe’s has plenty of design options, allowing you and one other person to enjoy a simple meal while basking in your yard’s beauty.


Fire Pits and Patio Heaters

An outdoor fire pit is essential if you want to wow your guests and help them warm up, too. Of course, you can instead use a gas or electric patio heater for a more portable option. These appliances come in a variety of size options and emanate heat to those who are nearby. A great fire pit option is a gas fireplace made of composite. This choice promises durability and can be manufactured to look either rustic or modern. If you want an effective, old-school option, opt for our wood-burning fire pits. Some homeowners prefer directly stoking the flames and may choose a wood-burning fire pit for cooking, too.


Snow Blowers

Using the best snow blower can make or break how comfortable your frigid days are. If you want a powerful appliance, use a gas snow blower, perhaps one that’s self-propelled to make the job even easier. If you get dark winter days where you live, choose a snow blower with headlights to help you clear your yard of snow more safely and comfortably. You might also just be looking for snow blower parts, in which case you can buy some shear pins to keep your engine intact. Or you might want to purchase a cover to protect your snow blower.


Outdoor Kitchen Appliances

Many homeowners dream of having an outdoor kitchen that can prepare many decadent backyard meals. If this sounds like you, take a look at our selection of charcoal grills, which offer a classic barbecue experience. You can also enjoy the convenience of a smart-compatible charcoal grill, allowing for a modern approach to traditional options. An outdoor fridge is also a great idea, whether you want extra refrigerator space or a nearby place to store drinks while entertaining guests.


Decorate Your Outdoor Space

No matter the season or holiday, Lowe’s has the outdoor decorations that’ll make your yard the envy of the neighborhood. From small touches like garden flags and yard statues to Halloween inflatables and outdoor Christmas lights, you can find the perfect decorations for the season.


Enjoy the Outdoors With Lowe’s

You should be able to enjoy every part of your property, including the outdoors. If you’re looking to optimize your backyard’s space, browse Lowe’s extensive outdoor product selection. Don’t know where to start? Talk to a store associate at your nearest Lowe’s location or send us a chat message on our website. We’ll pair you with options that are best for you and your backyard.
