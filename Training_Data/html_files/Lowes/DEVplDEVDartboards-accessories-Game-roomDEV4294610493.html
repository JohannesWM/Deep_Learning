Frequently Asked Questions


Q: How do you stabilize a dartboard?

A: To stabilize a dartboard, be sure to follow the manufacturers instructions. For maximum support, mount the dartboard into a stud in the wall. Refer to our guide How to Find Wall Studs to locate a stud thatll work. Make sure to install the dartboard at the proper height. Regulation dartboard height is 5 feet 8 inches from the floor to the center of the bulls-eye.


Q: Whats best to put behind a dartboard?

A: Its best to put heavy-duty anchors directly behind a dartboard if youre installing it into drywall. To protect the wall immediately surrounding the dartboard, its a good idea to install a dartboard backer. You might use a wood, cork or foam backer or a backer in a combination of protective materials. You want to choose a material that stops darts without damaging them.


Q: How thick should a dartboard backer be?

A: A dartboard backer should be a full inch thick to provide optimal protection. Anything thinner than that may not be sufficient to prevent stray darts from piercing through to the wall behind the backer.
