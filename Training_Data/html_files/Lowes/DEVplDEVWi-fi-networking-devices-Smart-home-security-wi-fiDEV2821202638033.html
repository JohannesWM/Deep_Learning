Get a Strong Connection With Wi-Fi and Networking Devices

Wi-Fi routers pair with modems to broadcast a Wi-Fi signal that allows Wi-Fi-enabled devices, such as computers and smartphones, to connect to the internet.


If a traditional Wi-Fi router isnt providing a strong enough connection for your household, a Wi-Fi extender can extend its range. Most Wi-Fi extenders work with any internet provider and can pair to any router. Some extenders feature smart lighting tools that detect the strongest points of connection in the home.


Mesh Wi-Fi systems utilize two or three units to establish a stronger connection for high internet use across several rooms of a home. One of the units connects to the modem and communicates with the other units to create a web of strong internet connections. Mesh Wi-Fi systems are available from trusted brands like Google and NETGEAR.


A cell phone signal booster boosts the cellular signal on cell phones, smartphones, tablets and hot spots. You can utilize a booster to increase your cellular signal from any phone company, including Verizon, AT&T or T-Mobile.
