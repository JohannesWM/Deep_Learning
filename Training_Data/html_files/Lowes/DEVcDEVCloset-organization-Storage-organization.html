Closet Storage and Organization 

Explore closet storage solutions that work best for the space that you have. 

Make your closets work for you with closet storage and organization solutions that fit your space. Whether you need an entire closet organizer or a single clothes hanger rack, Lowe's has a variety of products for your needs. 


Creating Custom Closet Storage

If out-of-the-box closet organizers don't work for your space, create your own perfect storage system. Lowe's offers products from popular closet organization brands such as ClosetMaid, RubberMaid, Easy Track and Rev-A-Shelf so you can choose from pre-assembled wire and wood closet kits or customize your own closet piece by piece. If you're looking for a new closet rod, browse Lowe's selection of various lengths, materials and colors to find what will fit your closet best. 



Clothes Hanger Racks and Portable Closets

If your home is low on storage space, consider a free standing closet organizer or clothes hanger rack to accommodate your wardrobe overflow. Depending on your storage needs, there are clothing racks with a single bar or racks that have shelves, hooks and multiple rods. Choose from racks that have wheels or are fully collapsible for maximum portability. Keep clothes dust-free when they're in storage with clothing racks that come with a cover.


Closet Storage Bins

Keep non-hanging closet items tidy with storage bins and baskets. Decorative water hyacinth or sea grass bins keep things tidy in plain sight, while plastic bins are great for when you need storage that is air or water-tight. For children's toys, consider a colorful toy caddy that will look great on its own in a play area. Lowe's offers bins designed to fit into cube storage units as well as collapsible bins that can be easily stored when you don't need them.


Shoe Organization

Shoes are notoriously hard to keep organized, but with the right closet storage solutions, Lowe's makes it possible. No matter what size your shoe collection is, you can find a multi-tier shoe rack to accommodate it. Keep shoes organized and out of sight with a shoe cabinet with a look that matches your home décor style. Over-the-door shoe organizers save space while keeping your footwear all in one place. Hanging shoe organizers take advantage of any closet rod space you have left over and keep shoes from cluttering the floor of your closet. 


Closet Accessories

Once you've chosen or created the closet storage system that works best for you, choose the hangers that will keep your clothes looking their best. Prevent clothing from ending up on the closet floor with non-slip grip hangers. Maximize your closet space with thin, velvet hangers that take up less space than traditional plastic ones. Hangers with clips are best for hanging pants and skirts, and there are even hangers to help hold your favorite accessories like scarfs, belts, ties or handbags. If you need a place for your guests to rest their bags when they're visiting from out of town, a luggage rack is a simple solution — it can be easily folded and tucked away when you don't need it. Looking to display a single special outfit? A clothing mannequin can serve as both a functional piece and a decorative one.
