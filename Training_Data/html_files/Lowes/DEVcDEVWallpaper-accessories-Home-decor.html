Bring Your Walls to Life With Wallpaper

Looking to give your room a new look with stylish wallpaper? Lowe’s is happy to be your wallpaper store. Whether you want to wallpaper a small bathroom or a large living room, our wallpaper selection has plenty of options to perfectly complement your home’s décor. Our options include simple stick-on wallpaper, pretty flower wallpaper, bird wallpaper and chinoiserie wallpaper, as well as color-based selections, like pink wallpaper or purple wallpaper.


Types of Wallpaper

Want a wallpaper that’s easy to apply and remove? Self-adhesive wallpaper, or peel-and-stick wallpaper, is designed for easy application. Simply peel off the back and apply the sticker wallpaper on a clean, dry wall. If you’re renting or looking for a wallpaper that you can remove with little trouble or damage, this is your best option. Other wallpaper options include prepasted, which can be activated with water, and nonpasted, which is traditional paper with no adhesive. But if you’re looking for temporary wallpaper, self-adhesive wallpaper is the way to go.


Our textured and decorative wallpaper options include papers that offer the look of tile, wood, stone, fabric or vinyl. You can find brick-look wallpaper by searching “red wallpaper” on Lowes.com. Textured wallpaper is commonly used in living rooms, such as stone wallpaper around a fireplace. It’s a more cost-effective option than adding real wood or stone. Grass cloth wallpaper is also a popular textured option that looks great in almost any room. 


Paintable wallpaper can be left as is or painted the color of your choice. It’s commonly used in small spaces, like a hallway, but can also be used in rooms and on ceilings.


Looking specifically for wallpaper for kids? We carry a variety of patterned and mural wallpaper with prints and themes ranging from butterflies and polka dots to trucks and Disney scenes. There’s also washable wallpaper that can be safely cleaned with warm water and soap, making it ideal for a child’s room, bathroom or kitchen. Vinyl wall decals and wall décor stickers are also popular. Or take the fun to the next level with white wallpaper that can be used as a mural for your child’s art and removed when it’s time to transition to a tween room.


Before you start a home wallpaper project, make sure to measure your walls and gather all the necessary application tools, including a putty knife, sponge, ladder, razor and cutting mat. If you’re looking for a how-to guide, you’ll find plenty of videos and inspiration under the Ideas tab at the top of the page.
