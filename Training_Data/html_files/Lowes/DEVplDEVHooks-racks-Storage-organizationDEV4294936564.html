Maintain a Neat Home With Hooks and Racks

Hooks and racks are important elements when it comes to decorating your home. Not only do you want to make sure what you choose is stylish, but you also want it to be functional. Lowe’s offers a wide variety of hooks and racks, from freestanding options to pieces you hang on the wall.
 

If you’re someone who entertains a lot, you may want to create a place at the door for guests to deposit their jackets, bags and hats. A coat rack on the wall could make the perfect addition. You can choose from racks with multiple hooks or options designed with shelves for extra storage.


If wall space is limited or you’re working with a small entryway, a coat hanger stand might be the best option to keep things organized. For larger entryways, consider a hall tree with a bench, hooks and cubbies.


Need to hang brooms in a closet or backpacks in the mudroom? Adhesive hooks, such as Command hooks, are a convenient way to add quick hanging storage to your home and can be placed virtually anywhere.


No matter what you need to hang, you can count on Lowe’s to have the solutions you need, from wall hooks to swag hooks and even saddle racks, too.
