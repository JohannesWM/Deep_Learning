Frequently Asked Questions


Q: Whats the best thing to clean a boat with?

A: The best cleaner is one that removes a wide range of stains and dirt without damaging your boats surfaces, furnishings or materials. At Lowes, we offer a broad selection of boat cleaners.


Q: What products do you use to clean a boat?

A: To clean a boat, you want a cleaner that can remove salt deposits, oil and other grime on the hull, as well as return the sparkle to your deck and furnishings.


Q: Is Dawn dish soap good for boats?

A: While Dawn dish soap can effectively remove many tough oil and grease stains, it might damage wax or polish on your boat. Its better to choose a specially formulated boat cleaner that you can be sure is safe and effective.
