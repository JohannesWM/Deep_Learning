Shop by Toilet & Bidet Innovations

Smart Compatible 

Automatic and intuitive features make smart toilets a more comfortable and user-friendly alternative to traditional units.  

Automatic Flush 

Sensors detect the users departure and automatically flush the toilet for hands-free operation and an always-clean bowl. 

Heated Seat 

Heated toilet seats provide increased comfort, eliminating the need to make contact with a cold surface.

Self Cleaning 

Special coatings and flushing mechanisms inhibit germ growth and buildup so the toilet bowl stays sparkling. 

Deodorizing 

Scented seats help mask unwanted odors so the bathroom always smells fresh and clean. 

Night Light 

Built-in night lights allow you to avoid using overhead lighting that may disrupt your sleep cycle.

Find the Best Toilet for Your Bathroom at Lowes

While purchasing a new toilet might seem like a simple task, toilets come in a variety of different styles, sizes and shapes. They also offer different features, so its important to find the one thatll best fit your needs. Whether youre looking for commercial toilets or want luxuries like heated toilet seats for your home bathroom, Lowes has you covered.


Different Toilets, Different Needs

Different toilets have unique features some may be important to you, while others arent. Do you need a tall toilet to comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act or a corner toilet to fit a small space? Do you have a preference between a one-piece vs a two-piece toilet? Maybe youre looking to add a bidet to your bathroom. Japanese toilets are multifunctional, with a traditional toilet and bidet capabilities built-in. Composting toilets are environmentally-friendly and eliminate the need for conventional plumbing. Wall-mounted toilets are a sleek and modern addition to any bathroom.


Toilet seats usually arent included with your toilet. Youll need to look at different toilet seat sizes and choose a seat that fits your toilet and complements your bathroom dcor. Elongated toilet seats or slow-close toilet seats are options that could work well in your home, depending on your style and requirements. Not interested in a toilet seat? An open-front toilet may be best for you.


Finally, tie your oasis together with toilet storage and matching toilet and bathroom accessories. You can even count on us for your toilet bowl cleaner and toilet paper needs.
