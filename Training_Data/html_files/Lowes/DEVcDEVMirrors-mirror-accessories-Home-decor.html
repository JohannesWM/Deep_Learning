Find the Perfect Mirror for Every Room in Your Home

Whether you need a mirror for function, style or both, weve got you covered with a diverse selection of styles and sizes for any space. Mirrors can make rooms appear larger, bring light to dark hallways and serve as a focal point for any room. Looking for a large wall mirror to hang above a fireplace? Simply need a small circular mirror to place above a bathroom vanity? On Lowes.com, you can shop by size, shape, style and more to easily find what youre looking for.

Unique Mirror Shapes
A round mirror is a classic option that always looks great in bathrooms and other small rooms. But if youre looking for something that offers a more unique look, consider some other shapes like irregular geometric mirrors, decorative sunburst mirrors and mirrors with arched tops. No matter what style youre going for, we have an option to complement your space. And keep in mind that a decorative mirror is a great substitute for artwork.

Functional Mirrors
No bedroom is complete without a full-body mirror, also known as a full-length mirror or floor-length mirror. Whether you want a standing mirror that can support itself or prefer mirrors over the door, we have both options. A door mirror is ideal if youre trying to save floor space in a room or have a small space to work with, like a dorm room. If big mirrors arent a problem for your space, opt for a large leaning mirror that can be placed against a wall or a stylish stand mirror.

An Array of Mirror Styles and Colors
Our mirrors come in a variety of frame materials and colors, including rustic reclaimed wood, modern metal, glass, gray mirrors and more. If you want to customize a mirror, we also carry mirror frame kits. Whether your style is modern, industrial, midcentury or farmhouse inspired, weve got a decorative wall mirror to tie your space together.
