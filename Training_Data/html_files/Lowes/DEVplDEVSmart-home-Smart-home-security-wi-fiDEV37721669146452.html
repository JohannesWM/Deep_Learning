Make Your Home a Smart Home


At Lowe's, it's easier than ever to make your home a smart home. With a range of smart solutions that connect to your smartphone or other smart device, you can access your systems with only a touch or two. Check your security systems, adjust your heating, turn your lighting on and off, monitor water systems for leaks and more — all without leaving your chair.


Smart home devices give you added flexibility so you can access your home's systems quickly and easily, while the devices learn your patterns and adjust their settings in response to your schedule. You can also save money on heating by programming your smart thermostat to reduce the temperature at night or while you're at work.


A smart home system integrates with your life and schedule to optimize your home when you're around and conserve resources when you're not. And with additional security features that let you connect your doorbell camera to your device, you also gain greater peace of mind.
