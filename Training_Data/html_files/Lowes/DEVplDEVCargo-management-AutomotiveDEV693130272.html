Flatbed Tool Boxes and Carriers for Your Cargo


If you frequently find yourself transporting tools, gear or materials in your vehicle, we have automotive organization solutions at Lowe’s that can make your life easier. Truck and flatbed tool boxes fit in the bed of a pickup truck or the back of an SUV and can hold all of your carpentry tools, hunting gear or camping equipment.


Going camping or taking a road trip? A cargo carrier can help you pack everything you need when your trunk’s storage space is lacking. Consider using a cargo bag to further protect the gear in the carrier from the elements. We also carry bike racks for transporting your bike to trails or races.


Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What are the uses for truck tool boxes?

A: You can use truck tool boxes to hold tools in the back of a pickup truck or SUV. You can also use these boxes to hold gear for outdoor activities, such as hunting, fishing or camping. The tool boxes will help you organize your gear and prevent it from moving around as you drive.


Q: What’s a hitch cargo carrier?

A: A hitch cargo carrier is a carrier about 3 to 4 feet high that attaches to your vehicle via its receiver or trailer hitch. Your cargo is then more easily accessible than if you stored it in a roof carrier.


Q: How do you secure cargo in a truck bed?

A: There are a few ways you can secure cargo in a truck bed to prevent the cargo from moving around or falling out. Adding a cargo carrier or flatbed tool box can help you organize gear and keep it in place. You can also use a truck bed liner with a grippy surface that’ll keep cargo from sliding, or you can tie things down with bungee cords, rope or chains.
