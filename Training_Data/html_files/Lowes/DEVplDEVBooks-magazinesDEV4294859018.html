Learn New Skills From Project Books

A quick internet search or online videos can be helpful when you're tackling a DIY project, but some people retain information better when it comes from books. Lowe's has a variety of home improvement, planting and carpentry books that can guide you through the steps to develop a new hobby or build a wooden desk.


If you're interested in building a specific piece of wooden furniture but aren't sure where to start, carpentry books often have instructions and patterns that you can refer to. There are books related to large-scale projects as well, such as designing and building a deck or an entire house or remodeling your existing home.


Indoor and outdoor plants all have different needs, and what helps one succulent thrive can be harmful to vegetable plants. A planting book about the plants you're cultivating can help you learn about what they need and what they don't so you don't make simple mistakes like overwatering.


Frequently Asked Questions  


Q: Can You Learn Carpentry From a Book? 

A: Carpentry books can be a great resource for anyone aspiring to be a self-taught carpenter. The right books can teach you about different carpentry techniques, styles and tools. And, some books even provide traceable patterns and step-by-step instructions for building specific pieces.


Q: What Are the Basics of Planting?

A: The basic steps for planting will depend on what you are trying to cultivate. Reading a planting book can be a good way to learn more about the plants you want to grow, including the types of environment and maintenance they require. No matter what plant you are looking to grow, you'll need the appropriate type of soil and the right size container. You'll also need to understand how much sunlight and water the plant needs.
