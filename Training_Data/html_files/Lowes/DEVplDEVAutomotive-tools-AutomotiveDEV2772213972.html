When it comes to automotive repair, using the right tool for the job not only saves time and hassle, but helps ensure that the work is done properly and safely. Shop Lowes large selection of automotive tools to find just what you need to repair your vehicle correctly. 


Whether youre looking for hose clamp pliers, a battery terminal tool or an oil filter wrench, find an array of mechanical tools to choose from. We have a wide selection of jacks and jack stands including scissor jacks, pallet jacks and bottle jacks as well as pullers to remove parts like bearings, pulleys and gears. We also carry remote diagnostics devices. 


Our mechanic tool kits feature specific tools in a range of sizes, such as wrench sets, as well as collections of assorted tools. Lowes also carries brake service kits, oil pressure check kits and ball joint service kits that contain all the tools needed for those specific jobs.
