Securing Your Home with Window Hardware

Window hardware safely prevents windows from being opened from the outside and provides secure fastening for outdoor decorations like window boxes. You can add beauty and security to the outside of your home with a variety of hardware materials that coordinate with any aesthetic. And to maintain your existing windows, including the window sash and all other parts of a window, Lowe's offers many types of replacement hardware to choose from.


Keep your family safe with window locks or security bars. Window locks prevent the opening or closing of your window from the outside and can help protect children from potential hazards. Security bars come in a wide array of materials and can be used for residential and commercial use. The sturdy structures fasten to the outside of windows to prevent intruders from opening or breaking a window in order to enter your home.


Window box brackets provide a firm hold for your window boxes and include stainless steel hardware for installation. At Lowe's, you can choose from many sizes of window hardware and window parts to fit an array of window types and measurements.
