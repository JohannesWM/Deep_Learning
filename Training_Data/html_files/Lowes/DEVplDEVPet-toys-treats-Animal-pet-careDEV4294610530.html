Keep Your Pups and Kitties Happy With Pet Toys and Treats


A household with dogs and cats wouldn’t be complete without pet toys and treats. Whether you’re replacing worn-out toys or purchasing playthings for a new pet, you can shop Lowe’s for a wide selection of toys to provide them with exercise and to keep you both entertained.


Our plush dog toys come in an assortment of amusing designs. Look for squeaker or crackle elements to get your pet’s attention and to add an extra level of fun. We also have a number of chew, tug and rope toys made of a variety of materials and an array of interactive toys to stimulate their minds. Dog chew treats include rawhide and non-rawhide chews as well as dental treats.


For your feline companion, we offer plenty of cat toys they can chase and bat around, including feather poles and mouse figures that stimulate their hunting instincts. Or choose a tunnel or maze to satisfy your cat’s curiosity and desire to explore.
