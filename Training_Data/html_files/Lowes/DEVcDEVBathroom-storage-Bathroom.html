Bathroom Storage Solutions

At Lowe’s, we offer bathroom storage solutions that accent your décor and provide plenty of storage space.


Vanities

Create stylish storage beneath your sink with a vanity. Double- or single-sink vanities can enhance your bathroom décor as they add storage space. Design styles include modern/contemporary, transitional, traditional, rustic, Mission/Shaker, farmhouse, casual, classic and coastal. Choose from features that include mirrors, sinks, adjustable shelves, integrated electrical outlets, soft-close drawers and soft-close doors.


For a finishing touch, consider using an attractive vanity tray to store small items like cosmetics and cotton balls. Vanity tray accessory kits, cosmetic organizers and hair dryer holders are also available. Choose a satin, matte, brushed or polished finish.
 

Medicine Cabinets

Situated over a vanity or sink, medicine cabinets store some of your bathroom’s most important contents and are ideal bathroom organizers. They’re available in many designs. With options from rustic medicine cabinets to modern medicine cabinets, you’re sure to find one to match your décor.


Choose from a surface style that attaches to the wall or a recessed cabinet that installs within the wall. Other options include over-the-toilet storage units and corner bathroom cabinets. Select from a variety of shapes — including rectangular, square, oval, arched and round.


A medicine cabinet mirror can have a single view, a biview or a triview. Another option is a sliding mirror. Or you can select a medicine cabinet without a mirror.


Something else to consider is how many bathroom shelves you’ll need. You can find cabinets with up to 12 shelves or no shelves at all. There are also over-sink shelves.


Other features include lighting and soft-close doors.


Wall-Mounted Cabinets

If your bathroom is large enough, consider wall-mounted cabinets. At Lowe’s, we offer options with closed doors, open shelves or a combination. Styles include transitional, modern/contemporary, traditional, farmhouse and mission/Shaker cabinets. And construction materials include medium-density fiberboard (MDF), wood, particleboard, metal and glass.


Shelving

Create vertical space with shelving. Choose from thousands of bathroom shelves in various designs at Lowe’s. Material options range from sleek glass to warm wood to beaming brass.


Shelves come in wall-mounted and freestanding styles. Some models have towel bars, and many come with mounting hardware. A good option for a small bathroom might be a space-saving over-the-toilet model with three shelves. Choose an open-shelf model to show off accent pieces or select a closed-cabinet design that keeps towels and other supplies out of sight.
