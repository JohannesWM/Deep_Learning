Types of Concrete

 

All Purpose

Use for building or repairing anything out of concrete, such as sidewalks, curbs, steps and patios.

Fast Setting

Use for applications such as setting posts. Sets in 20 to 40 minutes without mixing.

High Strength

Use for applications over 2 inches, such as sidewalks, driveways, patios and ramps.

Crack-Resistant

Use for applications over 2 inches, such as sidewalks, driveways, floor slabs and shed bases.

Sand Mix

Use to patch and repair concrete or as a sweep-in filler for pavers. Mixes with water.

Hydraulic

Use for stopping the flow of water through breaks in concrete or masonry walls and floors. For interior/exterior use.
