Find the Right Stair Parts for Every Project


Stair-Building Materials for Your Home Project

Whether you need to upgrade or fix your staircase, Lowe’s carries an extensive selection of stair materials, from banisters and newel posts to treads and more.

Wood stair treads are common, but you can set yours apart by choosing ones made of Brazilian cherry wood. Not only do they look great, but they’re also durable and termite-resistant. If you’re new to building projects, then purchase stair post installation kits. They’ll ensure that you have all the materials and information you need. Stainless steel handrail brackets will also tie your project together while minimizing the chance of discoloration. No matter what kind of staircase design you’re going for, we can help bring your vision to life.


Staircase Kits

For some builders and do-it-yourselfers, choosing every stair component, piece by piece, might take too much time. That’s why they might prefer staircase kits, which come with building materials that are manufactured to fit perfectly with each other. If you like the look of a modern staircase, black spiral staircase kits are a great option. They’re also space efficient and don’t need side support. Gray modular staircase kits are also fantastic options, as they generally can support more foot traffic and look less busy.


Stair Railings

Stair railings, also called step rails, are necessary for ensuring the safety of those climbing stairs and for framing your staircase’s aesthetic. Consider purchasing wood stair railings, which offer a natural finish while looking polished. However, you might want unfinished wood railings, which give you plenty of freedom to prime and paint however you’d like. Or you might be specifically searching for koto wood handrails, a light-colored building material with straighter woodgrain. They hide scuffs and dirt better and will function as subtle additions to your staircase. However, you might prefer metal railings, which are incredibly durable and easy to maintain. Choose white metal railings to get the same benefits — a subtle option against light walls.


Stair Balusters

Balusters are upright support beams. In addition to providing support for stair railings, they prevent people, pets and objects from falling through the space that between the treads and handrail.


Stair Nosings

To prevent people from slipping on your stairs and increase your stair steps’ life span, use stair nosings. Bamboo stair nosings are lightweight and shock-resistant, perfect for homes in earthquake-prone areas. They also come in a variety of colors, allowing you to take advantage of their benefits while allowing for plenty of customization. Vinyl nosings are also versatile options due to their affordability, durability and variety in design choices. If you still want the natural finish of wood with the advantages of vinyl, choose nosings made of wood veneer and a vinyl core. There are plenty of wooden finishes to choose from, letting you enjoy an affordable option with a high-quality and classic look.


Stair Risers

Stair risers are a basic component of any set of stairs. They’re the vertical part of the staircase that creates the height between each step. Risers come in a range of colors and materials


Stair Treads

People will first notice and step on stair treads. They also make a huge difference in a stair’s overall durability. Lowe’s selection of stair treads includes rubber stair treads. These are great choices for homeowners who prioritize material resistance, since rubber material has great shock-absorption capabilities and won’t chip or break. Laminate stair treads are also visually adaptable options that are very resistant to abrasion and denting.
