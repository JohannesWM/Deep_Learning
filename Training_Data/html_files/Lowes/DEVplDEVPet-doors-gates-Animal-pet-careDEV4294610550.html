Protect Your Home and Your Pet With a Dog Door

A dog door or cat door ensures that your pet stays safe in your home while having access to certain areas. Whether you want to give your pet access to the outdoors or keep them safe in one room of the home with a gate, the products in this collection help you create a pet-friendly home for your furry friend.


First, consider a pet door that you can affix to your exterior door to allow your pet easy access in and out of the house. Sliding pet doors fit existing sliding doors, feature a sturdy aluminum design and promise easy installation. Choose from a variety of sliding doors depending on your pet's size. Or, consider a flap-style dog or cat door that gives your pet freer access to the outdoors.


The collection at Lowe's also includes gates, such as a dog gate with door that lets you keep your pet contained in one room. These sturdy metal gates include user-friendly features like a lever handle with a one-touch safety lock, expandable sizing and convenient tension mounting. They serve as an alternative to a kennel when leaving your pet at home.
