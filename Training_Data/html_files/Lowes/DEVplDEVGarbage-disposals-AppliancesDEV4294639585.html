Frequently Asked Questions


Q: How long do garbage disposals last?

A: With proper maintenance, garbage disposals typically last around 10 to 15 years.


Q: What are the different sizes of garbage disposals?

A: The standard kitchen drainage hole is 3-1/2 inches in diameter, which accommodates most garbage disposals. To figure out what size you need, determine the correct horsepower based on expected usage. If you have up to two people in your household, consider a 0.5-horsepower garbage disposal. For a family of four to six people, go with a 0.75-horsepower garbage disposal. For commercial settings or for use by six or more people, purchase a 1-horsepower garbage disposal.


Q: What can I clean my garbage disposal with?

A: Garbage disposal cleaners come in various forms — capsules, tablets or sprays — that you can purchase online or at your local Lowe’s. These foaming cleaners penetrate and break down the built-up residue in your disposal to remove odors.
