ADA Compliant Accessible Bathroom Solutions

Accessible bathrooms facilitate the use of the toilet, sink, shower, bath and vanity for everyone. Customizing an accessible home for your particular needs makes your house easier to live in day to day. For a public or commercial bathroom, look for ADA compliant bathroom fixtures that meet accessible design standards under civil law in the United States.


Automated or single-handled sinks and tub faucets are useful features in an accessible bathroom. Distributing grab bars in and around the tub or shower provides support and helps prevent slips and falls. Lowe's carries showers with installed seating as well as walk-in tubs for getting in and out of the bath with less risk.


ADA compliant toilets are slightly higher, making them easier to access for users with mobility issues. Grab bars are also useful around the toilet to aid with sitting and standing. For a wheelchair-accessible bathroom, sliding doors to enter the bathroom and shower make it easier to navigate a wheelchair or walking aid inside.
