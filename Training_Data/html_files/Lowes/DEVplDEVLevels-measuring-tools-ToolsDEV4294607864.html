Measuring Tools for Professional Results


Using the correct measuring tools will not only save you headaches but will help you achieve professional-looking results. For carpentry projects that require precise measurements, it can also be crucial for structural safety. At Lowes, we stock a large supply of precision measuring tools for both the professional and do-it-yourselfer from simple squares, chalk lines and tape measures to the latest in high-tech instruments.


A laser measuring tool like a distance measurer will quickly calculate distances with the press of a button. When you need to determine whether a surface is true horizontal or vertical, choose a simple I-beam or box beam level, or upgrade to a laser level that can be mounted on a tripod. We also offer combination level, straight edge and angle finder tools.


Choose from our selection of stud finders to locate framing studs in your walls. Some feature deep metal detection and wire warnings, while others can display the stud edges and center all at once. For additional tips on locating framing studs, check out our guide How to Find a Wall Stud.
