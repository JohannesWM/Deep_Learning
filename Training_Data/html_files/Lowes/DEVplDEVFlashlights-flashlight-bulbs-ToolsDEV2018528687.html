Flashlights for Every Household

Flashlights are essential for any household whether for an emergency kit or everyday use. Today, there are so many types to choose from, including headlamps, LED flashlights, small flashlights, tactical flashlights and more. Not sure which one is right for you? Here are some things to consider.

Measuring Brightness

The light output of a flashlight is measured in lumens. Light output varies considerably, so youll want to keep this in mind if you need long-range brightness. Our selection varies from double-digit to quadruple-digit options like 2,900-lumen flashlights. Looking for the brightest flashlight on the market? Opt for a tactical flashlight with a high lumen rating and durable build with materials such as aircraft-grade alloy metal.

LED Flashlights

An LED flashlight is a top choice for many households today as LED lights are typically brighter than other light sources. LED flashlights are ideal for a number of applications including DIY home projects, car repairs, camping, outdoor sports and more. They typically require fewer batteries than older flashlight models, and their batteries usually last much longer. Looking for a rechargeable flashlight? Many LED flashlights come in rechargeable models.

Hands-Free Flashlights

If you need a hands-free flashlight for work, camping or running, a headlamp is the way to go. Headlamps range in lumen power and come in a variety of brands and styles.

No matter how much power you need and how much youre looking to spend, we have a flashlight thatll do the job. Looking for a specific brand like a Maglite flashlight? Only considering a rechargeable option? We make it easy to find what you need quickly with online filters so you can shop by brand, brightness, price, power source, charge type and more.
