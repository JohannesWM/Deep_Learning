Frequently Asked Questions


Q: Whats the most common fishing gear?

A: The most common types of fishing gear are hooks and lines. Pick up a fishing rod, waders, line cutters, lures and a bucket for your catch.


Q: What kind of gear do you need for hunting?

A: The kind of hunting gear you need will depend on the type of game youre hunting and the conditions youre hunting in. General supplies include weather- and terrain-appropriate clothing and footwear, as well as a backpack. Youll likely want to carry first aid supplies and bug repellent. And youll need your rifles or hunting bows, as well as animal calls, decoys and knives for skinning and gutting.


There are also hunting cameras you can use.

And, before you go hunting, be sure that you have all of the necessary licenses and that youre hunting in season.


If you plan to camp out or hunt in remote places, you may need water purifiers, campfire materials and a solar charger for your phone, among other camping supplies.


Q: What fishing gear do beginners need?

A: The basic fishing gear necessary for beginners includes a rod and reel. Spin-casting rods are the most common and easiest to use. Fishing also requires fishing line, hooks, bait, pliers for removing hooks and scissors to cut line. You can also bring sinkers and bobbers.
