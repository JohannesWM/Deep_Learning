Keep Your Glass Surfaces Clean with Help From Lowe’s

Whether you need glass cleaner to clean your shower doors, home windows or car’s glass surfaces, we have all the supplies you need to keep glass clean and streak-free. Glass cleaners come in multiple forms and application types including liquid, spray, foam, gel, wipes and hose end sprayers. If you’re working on a small job like cleaning glass shower doors, opt for a glass spray or wipes. When tackling larger jobs, like cleaning outdoor windows, you may want to go with a heavy-duty option like Windex outdoor glass cleaner that’s designed with a spray bottle that attaches to a garden hose. In addition to Windex glass cleaning products, we also carry other options from trusted brands including Sprayway glass cleaner, Zep and more.
