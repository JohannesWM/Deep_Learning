The Best Countertop and Built-in Microwaves


Cooking Made Easy

Microwaves are an important part of most kitchens. They’re great for defrosting frozen foods, reheating leftovers or cooking entire meals. And at Lowe’s, we offer a wide selection so you can find the microwave that suits your needs and style. Your choice will likely depend on the layout of your kitchen and the features that appeal to you. Shop by brand or browse top-rated options.


Built-in Microwaves

Built-in microwaves can install in a wall or cabinet for convenience and ease of use.


Over-the-Range Microwaves

Many people like to install microwaves directly above their ovens — so much so that microwave wall oven combos are available. Each combo has a connected microwave and oven, sold as one unit.


Drawer Microwaves

Drawer or under-counter microwaves sit under kitchen countertops. Instead of having a door that swings open, this type of microwave pulls open like a drawer. You can install them either to be flush with other surfaces or to protrude slightly.


Countertop Microwaves

The most common type of microwave is one that sits on a countertop. You can search by rating on Lowes.com to find what other customers consider to be the best countertop microwaves.


Safety and Controls

Depending on how often you use your microwave and what you use it for, you may be interested in specific features. Some features help to ensure safety. Others add convenience or help with cooking calculations.


Prevent children from opening and playing with the device by choosing a microwave that comes with child safety locks.


Convenience cooking controls make it easy to make popcorn or defrost frozen food at the touch of a button. With these, you don’t have to figure out how long to cook something or at what power level on your own. If you bake frequently, consider a microwave with a melting or softening mode for preparing butter or other ingredients.


Sensor cooking controls detect the moisture that food releases as it cooks and adjust power levels as needed. This helps to ensure that each dish turns out well.


Other Features

Get two devices in one with a convection microwave oven that can cook in either a standard microwave mode or a convection mode that uses a fan to circulate hot air inside the device.


If you’re looking for a one-rack microwave, you can filter by that feature on Lowes.com. We also offer two-rack microwaves and standard devices without racks.


To ease the process of cleaning your microwave, consider options with self-cleaning and steam-cleaning features.


Also consider a microwave that has inverter technology. It offers consistent heat, so that your food defrosts and cooks more evenly.


Want a smart microwave that works with your connected home devices? Our selection includes smart microwaves that are compatible with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, Apple HomeKit and more.


The Perfect Size for Your Kitchen

At Lowe’s, you can find a microwave that fits well in your kitchen and is the right size for all of your cooking needs. Large microwaves typically take up more than 1.5 cubic feet of space, so you’ll want to ensure you have enough surface area or wall space before selecting a device of that size. Small or medium microwave sizes may be best for apartments or kitchens where space is at a premium.


Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What’s the difference between a microwave and a microwave oven?

A: These are synonyms you can use interchangeably to describe the same appliance. But most people call it a microwave.


Q: What’s a microwave used for?

A: A microwave can cook or heat food quickly, bringing convenience and versatility to your kitchen. Some models even offer controls specifically designed to help you defrost and melt foods.


Q: What’s the most reliable brand of microwave?

A: We carry a wide assortment of well-known and trusted microwave brands, including GE microwaves, Whirlpool® microwaves, Frigidaire microwaves, Samsung microwaves, LG microwaves, Panasonic microwaves and KitchenAid microwaves. You can shop by customer ratings on Lowes.com to find top-rated over-the range or countertop microwaves.
