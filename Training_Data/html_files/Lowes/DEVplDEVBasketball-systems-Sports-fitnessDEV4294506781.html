Add a Basketball Hoop to Your Play Area

A basketball hoop allows you to enjoy a pickup game right outside your home. Exercise is important for your health, and adding an outdoor basketball system combines physical activity and lasting entertainment. These basketball systems come with a variety of features and sizes for all age ranges.


In-ground hoops are installed by creating a hole and securing a portion of the pole in the ground using the anchor kit and cement footing. Portable basketball hoops have a hollow heavy duty plastic footing at the base, so moving or transporting the hoop is easy. To secure the basketball hoop at your preferred location, fill the plastic basin with either water or sand depending on the instructions.


And for the colder months or to include younger children, you can bring a basketball hoop inside your home. A mini basketball hoop is ideal for a playroom and is suitable for toddlers and small children. Most basketball hoops come with adjustable heights so that the system can adjust as the child grows. Wall-mounted hoops are versatile and go on the back of the door or top of a wall.
