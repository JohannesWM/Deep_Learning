Carry-On Luggage That Fits Your Itinerary


When it's time to jet away for the weekend, make sure you have quality carry-on luggage that fits everything you need to have handy. At Lowe's, look for luggage to suit all your travel needs, including softshell or hardshell totes, suitcase sets or travel bags, backpacks, laptop bags, and virtually everything in between.


We offer a range of colors so you can choose a bright hue such as purple, green or red that stands out for easy identification on the luggage rack. Or, choose an unassuming dark shade that goes with everything and doesn't stain. Get the colors and logos of your favorite NFL, MLB, NBA or NHL team to show your team spirit.


Selections include wheeled luggage for easy transport around airports and hotels. Some models have full swiveled wheels for added control. We also have luggage that locks to provide increased security and expandable handles for easy reach. We also carry bags with padded compartments for laptops and other fragile equipment, as well as models made from rugged and water-resistant fabrics.
