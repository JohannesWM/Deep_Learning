Wheelchairs and Mobility Aids


If you or a loved one have accessibility needs, Lowe’s has wheelchairs and mobility aids to ensure everyone can move around the home safely.


Mobility Aids


Mobility aids can include walkers, rollators or medical walking canes. To avoid having to bend down, purchase a reaching tool to access items or quickly retrieve something that’s fallen on the floor. To easily carry and move groceries or laundry, consider a rolling shopping cart.


Wheelchairs and Ramps


Make your home or automobile easily accessible for individuals who use wheelchairs. Whether you need a ramp for a wheelchair to access an automobile, entry or doorway, Lowe’s has fixed or portable ramps for your specific needs.


For those that need to access medical wheelchairs in a bathroom, we have some solutions. There are zero-threshold or barrier-free showers for wheelchairs that make it easy to get in and out. Other bathroom accessibility products include integrated or freestanding shower seats, grab bars, walk-in bathtubs and more.
