Provide Warmth With Underfloor Heating

Install an underfloor heating system to help warm your home during the cold winter months. With electric floor heating or radiant floor heating, you place a heated floor mat under your flooring material. Heat from the mat rises through the floor and subtly warms the room.

Shop with us at Lowes to find the best underfloor heating option for your home. Some systems require you to take out your floor or install a new one. Other floor heating systems can install on ceiling joints in the basement.

A heated bathroom floor is ideal for chilly mornings. You can monitor the temperature by purchasing a thermostat with your heated floors. A non-programmable thermostat simply shows you the current temperature. A programmable thermostat enables you to adjust the temperature.

Several different floor materials can work with underfloor heating. Tile made of porcelain is perhaps the best, thanks to its high conductivity and porosity. Stone is another solid choice, as it also conducts heat. The compatibility of wood flooring with underfloor heating depends on the type of wood. Engineered timber is generally best because it adapts well to temperature changes.

Ampex, FloorHeat and SunTouch are among the most popular Underfloor Heating brands. While those brands are the most popular overall, you will also find a great assortment from WARMUP, QuietWarmth and MAPEI.
A typical price for a Underfloor Heating is $360 but can range from approximately $1.99 to $3,131.
2-zone distribution panel, 1-zone distribution panel and Heat plate are among the most common types of Underfloor Heating. While common, customers also consider 3-zone distribution panel, Thermal reflecting foil and Air eliminator as options.
