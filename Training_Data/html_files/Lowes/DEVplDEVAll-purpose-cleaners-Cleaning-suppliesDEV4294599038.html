Disinfect Your Home's Surfaces With All-Purpose Cleaners


An all-purpose cleaner allows you to eliminate odors, stains and dirt in a gentle yet effective way. We offer a variety of options to address the surfaces of your home from many well-known brands including Lysol and Clorox cleaners. You'll find liquid and foam cleaners, wipes and powders which can be applied from a pump bottle, a spray dispenser or as an aerosol spray.


Depending on your specific needs and the area of your home, you can choose from abrasive, antimicrobial or bleach-containing cleaners. More gentle green, natural and hypoallergenic options are also available.


All-purpose cleaners are suitable for the many surfaces of your home that accumulate dirt, dust, grime or oil, including kitchen and bathroom counters, wood and tile floors, or mirrors and windows. Check out the guide How to Clean Windows for tips and tricks on how to use all-purpose cleaners to clean up your view of the outdoors and let in more sunlight.
