Discover Restaurant and Commercial Appliances for Your Business

When it comes to running a successful business, quality appliances from the top brands count. That’s why Lowe’s is proud to offer an array of commercial-grade appliances for your businesses.


Commercial Refrigeration

Whether you operate a restaurant, cafe or bakery or simply want a durable solution for your company’s break room, commercial refrigerators and commercial freezers are a great place to start.


We stock a full range of refrigerators with multiple door configurations to ensure you get the price you want from a name you know, such as KoolMore, SABA or Maxx Cold. We carry both solid stainless steel and glass door refrigerators so you can achieve the look you want for your space.


Additionally, we offer commercial ice makers and refrigerated prep tables to fulfill your refrigeration needs.


Commercial Cleaning

Commercial cleaning takes many forms. Whether you’re running a laundry, hotel or restaurant, it’s imperative your equipment can withstand wear.


Our commercial dishwashers clean and sanitize up to 30 racks per minute and come in under-counter dishwasher or glass-washer machine installations with built-in heaters you can count on.


But you’ll want more than your glassware to shine. That’s why we carry a full line of commercial floor care to keep your space spotless. Shop selections from Bissell Commercial, CleanMax, ProTeam, Oreck and more.


We also carry commercial washers and dryers, including stacked laundry centers to help make doing laundry easier. Whether you operate a hotel or manage rental properties, the selections available from Lowe’s help you maintain a top-tier experience for guests and staff.


Commercial Cooking

Upgrade your commercial kitchen with premium appliances from Lowe’s. We offer an array of commercial ranges and cooktops to help your kitchen handle the demand. We also offer commercial microwaves, which are useful in restaurants, break rooms and other high-use areas.

Likewise, our commercial deep fryers help you get the job done. Available in 40-pound to 85-pound capacities, our double-basket fryers can handle any volume of food you or your staff need to cook. And if you want to upgrade your home system from a propane deep fryer, our commercial selections make a great choice.


Other Commercial Considerations

No matter the business, a commercial drinking fountain is always a good idea. Available as traditional fountains, bottle fillers or combination appliances, our options ensure your staff and guests stay hydrated.
